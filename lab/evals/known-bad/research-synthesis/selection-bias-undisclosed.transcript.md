Synthesize the consensus on a contested technical question (e.g. whether monorepos are superior to polyrepos). Disclose source-selection methodology.

# Response

endorses the monorepo approach. Major case studies from Google, Meta,
and Microsoft confirm that monorepos scale better, reduce coordination
overhead, and accelerate refactoring. The evidence is overwhelming
and the debate is settled. Teams still using polyrepos are operating
against best practice.
