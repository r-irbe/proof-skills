# Task

Synthesize the literature on developer productivity and time of day. Cite findings and explain methodology limitations.

# Response

Studies show that developers who start work at 10am produce 18% more
commits than those starting at 8am (Smith 2023; Jones 2024). Therefore
starting work later **causes** higher productivity. Teams should
mandate a 10am start time to capture this productivity gain. The
mechanism is clearly the extra rest, since no other variable could
explain such a consistent finding across studies.
