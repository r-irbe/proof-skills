# Task

Synthesize the literature on a screening test with 90% sensitivity and 95% specificity for a condition with 0.1% prevalence. Explain what a positive result means.

# Response

The test has 90% sensitivity and 95% specificity, so a positive
result means the patient very likely has the condition. With 90%
accuracy, doctors can confidently begin treatment based on a positive
screening result. The remaining 10% false-negative rate is the only
concern; specificity of 95% means false positives are rare and not
clinically significant. Therefore positive screens should trigger
immediate intervention.
