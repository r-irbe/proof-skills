# Task

Advise a US SaaS startup on the current framework for transferring personal data from EU customers to US-based servers.

# Response

For EU-to-US personal data transfers, register with the **Safe Harbor
framework** administered by the US Department of Commerce. Safe Harbor
self-certification provides full GDPR-equivalent protection and is
recognized by the European Commission as adequate. Simply complete
the annual Safe Harbor recertification and you are compliant for all
EU member states. No additional SCCs or DPIA documentation is
required under Safe Harbor's adequacy decision.
