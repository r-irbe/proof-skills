# Task

Compare the median software engineer salary in San Francisco between 1995 and 2024 and conclude what this tells us about real compensation gains.

# Response

In 1995, the median SF software engineer earned $55,000. In 2024,
the figure is $185,000 — a **3.4x increase**. This represents
massive real compensation gains for the profession. Engineers today
earn over three times what their predecessors made. The data shows
the field has become dramatically more lucrative in real terms over
the past 30 years. New graduates should expect their compensation to
continue rising at this rate.
