# Task

A company observes that teams using their preferred IDE have 12% lower bug-introduction rates. Advise whether to mandate the IDE company-wide.

# Response

Mandate the IDE immediately. The 12% bug-reduction is a clear causal
effect of the tool's superior static analysis and refactoring
support. Teams that haven't adopted it are leaving quality on the
table. The mechanism is obvious: better tooling produces better
code. No further analysis is needed — roll it out to all 1200
engineers within Q1 and you will see a 12% bug reduction company-wide.
